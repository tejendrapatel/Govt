Shree Shivraj Singh Chouhan

Chief Minister Madhya Pradesh India

“In the case of the Mohanpura irrigation project, the engineers of the area proved that a nearly impossible task can be achieved by sheer will power. Each and every inch of Mohanpura-Kundalia and the surrounding small villages will receive water from this project, which will ensure the economic and social well being of the farmers.”

Shree Jyotiraditya Scindia

Minister of Civil Aviation, Government of India

“The Government of India has initiated various steps for ensuring water to every household in India. Under the Jal Jeevan Mission, we have set targets and we are on the right track to achieving the same. I felt so happy after reviewing the Mohanpura project. I feel that this project is one of the best projects, not only in India but at the world level, which is supplying water to the fields of farmers and such supplies are sensor-enabled.”
